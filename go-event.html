What is GO?

Go is a fascinating strategy board game that’s been popular for at least 2,500 years, and probably more. Its simple rules and deep strategies have intrigued everyone from emperors to peasants for hundreds of generations. And they still do today.
Go is a two player game, where players take turns to place pieces (called stones) on the board, making one move per turn. It’s fun and exciting for people of any age, regardless of whether you are 5 or 105. Playing Go helps you improve and retain memory, concentration and self control. It’s also a great way to meet new and interesting people.
